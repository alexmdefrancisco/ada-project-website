        <h2 class="subsection-title" style="margin-top: 0;">The Project</h2>
        <p>
          <strong>The Big Shot</strong> is an exploration of financial market behaviors in the periods preceding major global crashes. Inspired by the film <em>The Big Short</em>, we asked ourselves a simple question: are investors truly "blind" to impending disasters, or are the warning signs buried deep within the noise of daily data?
        </p>
        <p>
          By analyzing decades of stock and ETF data, studying volatility clusters, and mapping industry correlations, our goal is to determine if the market whispers before it screams. This website serves as both our analysis portfolio and a narrative journey through the history of modern financial crises.
        </p>
